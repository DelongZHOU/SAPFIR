Sherbrooke Alternative Protein Feature IdentificatoR (SAPFIR) seeks to understand how alternative splicing, transcription initiation and termination change the localization or function of a gene by regulating which localization signals, functional domains and other important protein features are present in the mature mRNA.

SAPFIR contains the following functions:

  • Single Gene Annotation
  • Enrichment Analysis
